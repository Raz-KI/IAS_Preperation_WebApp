About the App

The IAS Virtual Interview App is designed to help UPSC aspirants prepare effectively by simulating real interview experiences using AI technology. The app not only mimics the interview environment but also builds confidence by allowing users to practice answering questions in a realistic setting. After each session, candidates receive instant feedback and detailed performance reports that highlight their strengths and areas for improvement. By consistently practicing and reviewing feedback, aspirants can fine-tune their responses, improve communication skills, and boost their overall confidence.
